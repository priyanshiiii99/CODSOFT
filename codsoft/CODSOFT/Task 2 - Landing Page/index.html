Our Story

Inspired by the vibrant tea culture of India, Masala & Chai brings the authentic flavors and warm hospitality of traditional Indian cafes to the heart of the city. Our space is a celebration of India's rich culinary heritage and communal dining tradition.

Our founders, Priya and Raj, grew up in the bustling chai stalls of Mumbai and the serene tea gardens of Darjeeling. They bring generations of family recipes and tea blending expertise to create an authentic Indian café experience.

"We believe that great coffee brings people together, creates conversations, and makes ordinary moments extraordinary."

- Sarah & Michael, Founders
